# Evidence

This section documents observable evidence of privilege abuse.

---

## Audit Log Events

- Role assignment usage events
- Administrative actions performed by nonadmin users
- Timestamped changes recorded in Entra ID audit logs

---

## Observations

- Excessive privileges allowed actions beyond operational requirements
- No immediate alerts were generated for these actions
- Visibility was present, but proactive controls were limited

---

## Risk Summary

Privilege misconfiguration significantly increases the risk of:
- Unauthorized access
- Accidental misconfiguration
- Privilege escalation paths
