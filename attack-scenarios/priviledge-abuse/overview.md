# Privilege Abuse (Excessive Role Assignment)

This scenario demonstrates how excessive or misconfigured identity privileges can be abused to gain elevated access within an Azure environment.

The objective is to show how legitimate permissions, when poorly managed, can lead to unauthorized administrative capabilities without exploiting software vulnerabilities.

---

## Scenario Overview

A user account is assigned a role that exceeds its operational requirements.  
This misconfiguration enables the user to perform privileged actions that should normally be restricted.

The scenario focuses on identifying excessive permissions, validating abuse paths, and assessing associated security risks.

---

## Objectives

- Identify overly permissive role assignments  
- Demonstrate privilege misuse without exploiting vulnerabilities  
- Observe audit and activity logs related to role usage  
- Highlight the importance of least-privilege access  

---

## Expected Outcome

- Successful execution of privileged actions  
- Visibility of role based activity in audit logs  
- Identification of excessive permission exposure
